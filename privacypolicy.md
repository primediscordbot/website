# 3. Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: December 19, 2024

At **Prime**, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when using the Prime Discord bot. By using Prime, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use Prime, we collect the following types of information:

### a) User Data
Prime does not directly collect personal information like email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable details. However, when interacting with the bot, certain information may be stored temporarily to enhance functionality:

- **Username**: We may store your Discord username and user ID to provide bot commands.
- **Message Content**: Some messages sent to Prime may be stored to allow Prime to respond appropriately, like when using commands or interacting in text channels.
- **Server Information**: Prime may store information about the Discord server you use it in, such as server ID, channel names, roles, and other server settings necessary for bot operations.

### b) Data from Interactions
When you interact with Prime, we may collect data to improve functionality:

- **Commands Used**: Commands you invoke, and the results of those commands, may be stored temporarily to improve bot behavior.
- **Logging Data**: Logs such as command invocations, errors, and user interactions may be kept for performance monitoring and issue resolution.

We do not share or sell your data to third parties. However, we may share information in the following cases:

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. After this period, data may be deleted or anonymized unless otherwise required by law.

## 5. Your Rights

As a user of Prime, you have the following rights over your data:

- **Access**: You can request to view the data that Prime has collected about you.

- **Opt-out**: You can disable certain features of Prime in your server, which may reduce the amount of data collected by the bot.

To request access, deletion, or any changes regarding your data, please contact us using the contact information provided below.
